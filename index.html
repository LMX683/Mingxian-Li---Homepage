About Me

I am Mingxian Li, an undergraduate student at Sun Yat-sen University. Since 2024, I have been studying at the School of Integrated Circuits.

My current interests include multimodal reasoning, retrieval-augmented learning, and intelligent systems for chemistry. I am particularly interested in building robust reasoning pipelines that integrate vision, language, and external knowledge sources.

In parallel, I also work on microwave wireless power transfer systems, with a focus on system-level modeling and experimental validation for meter-scale and watt-level energy delivery.
